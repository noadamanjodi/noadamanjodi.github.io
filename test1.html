Citizens are advised to ensure that their mobile number is updated online in both Vehicle Registration and Driving Licence Registration to receive important alerts, OTPs, and service-related information.

For this service, there is no need to visit the RTO Office. Using the official Vahan and Sarathi government portals, mobile numbers can be updated easily from home.

These online facilities help vehicle owners and driving licence holders save time and avoid unnecessary visits to offices. The portals are user-friendly and accessible across devices.

Official Government Portals: https://vahan.parivahan.gov.in/mobileupdate https://sarathi.parivahan.gov.in
