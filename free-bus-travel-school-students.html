In a significant move aimed at promoting education and reducing school dropouts, the Government of Odisha has approved free bus travel for students across the state.

The initiative, part of the Mukhyamantri Bus Seva (MBS) scheme, replaces the earlier 50% concession with completely free travel on both non-AC and AC bus services.

The decision was formalised through a notification issued by the Commerce & Transport Department on December 24, 2025, following a high-level meeting chaired by Chief Minister Mohan Charan Majhi.

Students can avail the benefit by presenting a valid student ID card or wearing their school uniform while boarding. A zero-value ticket will be issued through the Electronic Ticket Issue Machine (ETIM).

The Odisha State Road Transport Corporation (OSRTC) has been directed to modify software systems and revise bus routes and timings to ensure maximum coverage of schools in both rural and urban areas.
