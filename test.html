NALCO Officer's Association Damanjodi Updates

The state government unveiled ‘Ama Sathi’, a unified WhatsApp chatbot that will make 120 public services available to more than 4.5 crore residents across the state. Developed by the Centre for Modernising Government Initiative (CMGI) in collaboration with Meta, the chatbot is accessible in both Odia and English via a single WhatsApp number: 9437292000.

Alongside this initiative, Chief Secretary Manoj Ahuja launched a dynamic dashboard for the Odisha Right to Public Services Act (ORTPSA) central monitoring system. He stated that the platform has been designed as a one-stop digital assistant offering citizens easy access to a wide range of essential public services.

Through the chatbot, citizens can conveniently raise and track public grievances, access housing and urban development resources, and interact with general administration services.

The chatbot also provides services related to education, agriculture, and horticulture, ensuring that farmers and students receive timely information and support. Users can also connect with police services for assistance, check land records, and obtain revenue-related details.
